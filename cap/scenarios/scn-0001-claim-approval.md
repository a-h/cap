# Claim approval

## Description

A claims handler submits a claim and the system decides whether it may be approved
automatically.

## Steps

- The claims handler submits a claim.
- The system evaluates the applicable policies against the claim.
- The system returns an automatic approval, rejection, or a referral to a human.
